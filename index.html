About

“Indoor football is a popular game played around the world, this fast paced sport is ideal for those who want to increase their fitness level, coordination, balance and agility in a friendly and social environment.”

Studiebold is an indoor soccer tournament for all higher education across Jutland and Funen. Here the teams meet each other through a series of final events. Magic with the ball, spirit on the court and coziness are at the heart of the Studiebold. The Danish Workers’ Sports Association is behind the tournament, which is being rolled out throughout the country.

Rules

The student football rules set is inspired by Futsal where no gangs are played, as you know it from regular indoor football. The ball is a bit heavier that makes the ball not jump unsteady. As a player, you have better control of the ball on the hard indoor court.

  • 7 minutes per match.
  • Girl goals count for 2.
  • The ball is a Futsal ball. It is a little heavier than usual.
  • 5 players on the field lier team - max 3 replacements
